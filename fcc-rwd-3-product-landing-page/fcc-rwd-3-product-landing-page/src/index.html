Frost Craft

The number one battle royale game!

Some devils suddenly appear on the face of earth and kill every last human except some who took refuge in a cave. These devils go underground and make home, Barren of all landscape, all of the Earth catches Frost. Everything is now frozen. One guy amongst the refugees is a scientist with a backup of secret experiments tested by government for these kinds of situations, only problem? They need to be crafted and its all out in the open. He gives a copy of document “How to Craft” to everyone and they all head out separately to try out their lucks.
